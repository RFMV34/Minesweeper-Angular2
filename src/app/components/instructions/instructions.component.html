Instructions

Simple Minesweeper JS game using Angular2. You win, when you reveal all fields/boxes without bomb. You lose when reveal at least one field/box with bomb. Have fun, or maybe not :-D.

Used libraries:
  • Angular2
  • MaterializeCSS (only CSS)
  • AnimateCSS (only CSS)
